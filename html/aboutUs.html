Our History

W.K. Kellogg established our company 117 years ago, in February 1906. As an early conservationist, prominent philanthropist, and pioneer of wellbeing, our esteemed founder was driven by a passion for people, quality, and innovation. His discovery of toasted flakes cereal revolutionized the industry and became globally renowned. Discover more about our journey, our products, and how we've been making everyday moments extraordinary for over a century by exploring the timeline below.
